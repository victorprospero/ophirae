Story of Ophirae

Embrace your insignificance... Accept your end

OPHIRAE is a death/black metal band formed in 2012 by competent musicians from the brazilian underground elite who prefer to keep their identities obscure, adding mistery to a melodic and extremely deep ambience.

After the success of the singles "Pride" and "Sloth", the band released the album "Ophidian" in 2022. Later released the third video clip "Crawl" and went to a brazilian mini tour alongside the americans of Toxic Holocaust.

The album "Ophidian" features the special participation of Astaroth (Besatt), Paolo Bruno (Thy Light) and Luiz Louzada (Vulcano) and the release was made in a partnership between Mutilation Productions and Black Metal Store.

Today the band is based in Portugal and is preparing the next album.
